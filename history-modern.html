Adoption patterns

Large organizations adopt Neo‑Swiss through centralized design systems. This includes tokenization (colors, spacing), responsive column rules, and component accessibility standards.

Case study cues

  • System tokens for spacing and color.
  • Component libraries (buttons, cards, forms) with clear states.
  • Documentation for designers and engineers (examples + constraints).

When executed at scale, Neo‑Swiss yields predictable and trustworthy interfaces suitable for enterprise customers.
