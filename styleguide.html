Typography scale

Fluid scaling with clamp(). Line heights tuned for readability and hierarchy. All fonts use Inter.

Body — clamp(16px, 2vw, 18px)

line-height: 1.6 | font-weight: 400

This is a body paragraph demonstrating the readable measure and line height. Neo‑Swiss prioritizes clarity through scale and generous whitespace, not decorative elements. The measure is constrained to 60–75 characters for optimal readability across screens.

Responsive grid

CSS Grid with 4 columns (mobile) → 6 (tablet) → 12 (desktop). Centered container with max-width 1200px.

Mobile (< 768px): 4 columns, gap 12px, padding 20px

Tablet (768px – 1200px): 6 columns, gap 16px, padding 36px

Desktop (1200px+): 12 columns, gap 24px, padding 24px
